##  Project Overview
Developed an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze Costa Coffee sales data and provide insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and store performance. The dashboard enables business users to monitor key metrics and make data-driven decisions.

##  Business Problem
Costa Coffee generates thousands of sales transactions, making it difficult to:
- Track overall sales and profitability.
- Identify best-selling and most profitable products.
- Compare performance across different store locations.
- Understand customer purchasing patterns.

##  Project Objectives
- Analyze sales, profit, and customer footfall.
- Compare performance across stores.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Discover peak business days and hours.
- Build an interactive dashboard for easy reporting.

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Cost, Total Profit, Footfall)
- Sales by Product Type
- Sales & Footfall by Store Location
- Orders by Weekday
- Hourly Order Trend
- Product Category Analysis
- Top 5 Profitable Products
- Interactive Filters (Store, Month, Day)

##  Business Impact & Insights
- Identified **Barista Espresso** as the highest profitable product.
- Found that **8 AM – 10 AM** is the busiest ordering period.
- Compared sales and customer footfall across all store locations.
- Analyzed weekday sales trends to support staffing decisions.
- Identified product categories contributing the highest revenue.
- Provided an interactive dashboard that simplifies business reporting and supports data-driven decision-making.
